Institution

SRI SAI RAM INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, one of the premier Autonomous institutions in TN is approved by AICTE,GoI.To add to its pride, the institution is recertified as ISO 9001:2015 Certified institution as well, the institution has been accredited with “A+” grade with the CGPA of 3.30 by the Executive Committee of NAAC. Besides, five departments(CSE, MECH, IT, EEE, ECE)have been accredited for 3 years by NBA. The institution has also reached 192th position amongst the Top 200 Engineering Institutions all over India in NIRF work by the MHRD, GoI. In addition, the college has ranked for 3rd in SWACHH CAMPUS RANKING 2018, conducted by MHRD,GoI all over India.The college has awarded as a best performing MHRD-(IIC) in the year 2018-2019 by MHRD Innovation Cell for achievements in Entrepreneurship activities done by the Institute.It has been consistently ranked among the top 15 engineering institutions under Anna University.

Department

The Department of CSE is accredited by NBA,AICTE and has acquired Autonomous status under Anna University, Chennai.Every year Department produces at least 10 rank holders and has Centre of Excellences for Red Hat, Cisco, Oracle, Salesforce, VMWare & Data Science and Big Data Analytics.The students of CSE were placed in top MNC companies like TCS,WIPRO,CTS,ZOHO,Amazon and so on.The Department has signed MoU's with ICTACT, Red Hat Academy, Cisco Academy, Oracle Academy, SANDS India Pvt. Ltd., EPR Labs, IBM Rational Seed, Network Bulls Pvt.Ltd., and CSN Systems. Our students are active members of CSI,ISTE, SIESRP and IEI.

Grab The Infinities

Description:
  • This technical event consists of 6 different programs and where each of the programs are interlinked to each other.
  • Initially the coder starts with a problem and if solves a problem then he will get a string output along with the next program he/she has to solve.
  • The clue or the solutions from the initial program is given as a test/custom input by the user to next program to get the next clue and next program he has to solve.
    So, this process continues until he solves the final program (6th one) and got the very last result revealed to the moderators.
  • The fastest hand wins the game.
  • All the coders must have "HackerRank "account and all the languages in HackerRank are allowed.

What is a Nutpam 2020?
It is a one day symposium conducted by the students of Computer Science and Engineering Department of Sri Sairam Institute of Technology.

Who can come?
Nutpam 2020 is open to any enrolled undergraduate or graduate student.

When is the event? How does registration work?
The event is on October 29, 2020 and the Registration will happen through Google Forms.
